About


Quizzing has become a sport of the modern times. It is a sport in which both the player and the audience quench their thirst for knowledge.


Madurai Quiz Society considers quizzing more an ART than a sport. Many metro cities have their own quiz clubs to take care of quizzing in their respective regions, and we so dearly felt the lack of one at Madurai.


Thus 2010 saw the birth of “The Madurai Quiz Society – The no I don’t know zone”, its founders being Sundarnathan D, Srimukundan R, Srinivas Arunachalam L, Siddharth S and Narendran C. The Madurai Quiz Society Trust is an officially registered Trust. Ours is the only officially registered group for quizzing in the region.


We are a bunch of avid quizzers who got attracted to each other by quizzing. We share one same ideal, one same thought and that’s what brought us together to start The Madurai Quiz Society.
