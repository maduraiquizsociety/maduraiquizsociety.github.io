November 2010

In-Quiz-itors'10 - The Birth

Read More Gallery


CHIEF GUEST:Mrs. Premalatha Paneerselvam, Senior Principal, Mahatma Schools, Madurai

GUEST OF HONOUR:Prof. R Rengaswamy, Secretary, Gandhi Memorial Museum


Our initiative was supported by the Gandhi Memorial Museum, Madurai. They were the title sponsors for in-QUIZ-itors 2010. We were also supported by Madurai Meenakshi and Mangayarkarasi Group, Tayub Textiles and Marina Hotels. We were given a helping hand by The Hindu and The Indian Express.

The Madurai Quiz Society was officially declared open by Mrs. Premalatha Paneerselvam, Senior principal-Secretary, Mahatma Schools, Madurai at The Gandhi Memorial Museum, Madurai on November 14, 2010. The in-QUIZ-itors 2010 was started off by Prof. R Rengaswamy, the Secretary, Gandhi Memorial Museum and Dr. T Ravichandran, the Director, Gandhi Museum, Madurai. The Quiz also had as its guests of honour, Mrs.Vijaya Sundar, Principal, Mahatma K K Nagar and Mr.Ramesh Rajagopalan, a famous quiz master in the Madurai Region.

The event saw to the attendance of about 30 schools and 78 teams from in and around Madurai. The written prelims paved way for the clash of the titans, the Finals. The battle of knowledge was accompanied by fun amongst the six finalists to win the coveted inaugural trophies and cash awards. The feedback from the parent and the Teacher fraternity was overwhelming.

And one occurrence, we should call the highlight of the event, Hon'ble Mr. Justice K. Chandru, Honourable Judge of the Madras High Court graced the event and addressed the enthusiastic quizzers. He spoke about encouraging children to come out and participate in more number of such events for overall development.
